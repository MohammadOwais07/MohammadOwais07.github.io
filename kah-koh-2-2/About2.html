OUR HISTORY

1905

1905 Since 1905 the Rizek family has been known for their passion in cacao production in the Dominican Republic. Their farms located in the heart of our country are distinguished for the excellence of it genetic material and the exclusive post-harvest treatment given to their fruits.

2012

2012 Our acceptation and growth allow us to open our first store in Bluemall, a place to expose chocolate creations and promote its culture to the heart of the Dominicans. Our creativity, talent and experience on gathering the best ingredients allow us to expand our base recipes: 55%, 62%, 70%, 82% and 100%.

2011

Kah Kow was introduced to the local market in 2011, with our recipes 70% and 62% we become pioneers in the gourmet chocolate industry by being the first certificated organic chocolate produce at origin.

2013

After releasing our chocolate and macadamia spread Chocodamia, we expanded our range of products with other organic and seasonal artisanal confections.
