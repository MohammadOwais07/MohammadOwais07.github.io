Odelle Nyse (ON) brings power back to the individual

Odelle Nyse

Odelle Nyse (ON) brings power back to the individual by enabling you to make data driven decisions towards behavioral change. The essence of one’s freedom is derived from internal liberty towards self-determining how one should express themselves in the world. ON’s purpose is to help an individual self- actualize into their self-determined best version. A human is the smallest structural and functional unit of any civilization. In other words, to build a great society, the individuals living within set society have to be empowered to become their self-determined best version. Self-actualized individuals play an important role within social organizations that creates products and services that reshape Social and Environmental forces towards improving Life Conditions and reducing Suffering.

Polyocracy

Polyocracy enables turned ON individuals to self-organize into both local and online communities; focused around building products and services that reduce suffering and improve life conditions. This mean Polyocracy is the decentralized system that autonomously and intelligently coordinates, while self-regulating, the trillions of micro-processes needed in creating and dispersing these products and services. This platform is a decentralized mobilization tool that seamlessly facilitate peer to peer social contract formation between individuals within teams. By building autonomous formal networks of collaboration, Polyocracy manages the synchronicities of all processes needed to efficiently reduce the cost of producing products and services that reduce suffering and improve life conditions.
