Synopsis

Matter Robotics applies current breakthroughs of additive manufacturing while pushing the envelope forwards through the creation of products and services that reshape Social and Environmental forces towards improving Life Conditions and reducing Suffering. The products and services created in this organization serve as modular pieces in an ever growing fleet of technologies that can be combines towards building systems that achieve a certain desired outcome for our clients.

Areas of Specialization

Energy

Matter Robotics is specialized in building the tools needed by Poly Energy to develop the energy products. This means that we don’t directly build the energy product but rather the technologies needed to realized the products Poly Energy offers.

Infrastructure

We offer a fleet of solutions to helping our clients (individuals, communities, governments, and other organizations) build the infrastructure needed. Products from our technologies range from single house to sky scrappers to entier streets and cities. Depending on the tier you choose we can offer combine our technologies along with Poly Energy’s to provide a complete experience. For maximum efficiency we recommend taking advantage of our personalized process of defining and fitting the technologies you need to your specific location in spacetime.

Food

Our last area of specialization is building Automated Farming Systems. These products range from building single robots that seed, fertilize, irrigate and harvest crops to entire fleets of drones and warehouses used for vertical farming. Just as the above areas, technologies found in this wing can be combined with other technologies
