FEATURES

360°Data Model of Your Organization

Using data gathered from all parties/nodes engaged in social contracts driven to manifest a certain products or services, our platform builds a holistic data model of their value stream. Such a model is used to help you understand the existing or needed web of interactions that are responsible for the current trajectory of creating set product or service. Perspective is everything, so this feature provides you a variety of points of view to be able to understand your organization so as to make data driven decision on points of improvement.

Synchronize Your Organization

Through the holistic data model, our system autonomously ensures that all nodes/parties of your value stream are up to date and speed on the information and requirements necessary for reaching their daily, weekly monthly and annual goals. This feature means that you'll no longer have to worry about syncing your supply and demand, it will be done autonomously and dynamically adapting to the market. Through this feature, Polyocracy thus becomes the decentralized system that autonomously and intelligently coordinates and syncs, while self-regulating, the trillions of micro-processes needed in creating and dispersing set products or services.

Actionable Suggestions

This feature embodies our Organizational Virtual Assistant (OVA) that analyzes data points gathered from your organization’s 360° data model, coordinating and syncing all nodes/parties of your value stream to provided actionable suggestions towards points of improvement in your organizations that efficiently reduce the cost of producing your products or services. These suggestions are cuscaded towards the right parties/nodes in your organizations that can apply set suggestion towards making set changes towards improving efficiency.

Process

How It Work

Facilitate Peer to Peer Social Contract Formation

Utilizing Social Contracts as a Service, Polyocracy takes the developments made in Odelle Nyse (ON) and facilitates the creation and maintenance of contracts anchored towards collaborating towards manifesting a certain product of outcome. The application of social contracts as a service is different in this platform given that it deals with the complexity involved in tracking and attributing the specific individual creations and innovations. Products and Services are made up of smaller functional modules that execute only one aspect of the desired functionality needed in the final product or service. These functional modules are created or served by specific parties/nodes of your value stream. Ergo, the value created by set party/node should be attributed and owned by set party/node. This is reflected upon the social contracts facilitated by Polyocracy.

Formal Networks of Collaboration

Once peer to peer social contracts are formed, our systems takes a macro view of the inter collaboration between specific parties/nodes of your value stream. The resulting network serves as the bedrock for building a 360° data model for the flow of information, resources and value throughout your organization. This formal network of collaboration not only helps the system but also every single party/node in it to realize their role and the importance of it given how it fits into the larger product and service. Our network consists of diverse and unique types of individuals and teams working together to perform one or more functions in the of value streams needed in the creation of set product or service.

Decentralized Mobilization Tool

Polyocracy is supposed to enable individuals and teams within organization muster then coordinate their actions towards building a set product or services that that reduce suffering and improve life conditions. Such self-organization can be in both local and online communities thus by passing previous limits to innovation through collaboration. Being a decentralized mobilization tool also allows for an inbuilt resilience to the networks that are engaged in building set product and service unlike in centralized forms of organizations that are rigid and have one single point of failure.
